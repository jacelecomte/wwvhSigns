Warren Woods Veterinary Hospital Grooming Consent Form

Your pet is very important to us. Please be assured that every effort will be made to make your pet as comfortable as possible, and because we care, it is required for them to be up to date on Rabies, Distemper with lepto (DHLPP) and Bordetella. If your pet is severely matted or tangled, there is a greater risk of injury. Even though all proper precautions will be taken, there is always a chance that nicks, or clipper burns can occur. In rare cases, pets with serious medical conditions can experience medical problems while grooming. These conditions are not always obvious. In signing this form, you fully understand that there are potential risks during your pets grooming session. Also by signing this form you agree to pay in full for services rendered including those deemed necessary for medical complications.
